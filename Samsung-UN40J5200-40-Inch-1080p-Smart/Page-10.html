5.0 out of 5 starsThe TV is supported just fine. Switching between cable/netfix/hulu/and internet is a breeze.
ByChristine Wigginson May 25, 2015
I bought this TV before there was any feedback and found the negative feedback before it was delivered. I must admit that, based on the prior feedback, I was concerned about the feet as I tried to assemble the TV. They seemed very flimsy, at first. However, it was not until I had fully screwed in the feet that I realized they were more than ample to support the television. In fact, the TV is quite secure. Yes, the feet are plastic, but the TV is very light and it does not require very much support at all. The picture is perfect. The sound quality is great. I am delighted with how easy it is to navigate between cable/netflix/hulu/internet. It was a great buy and I am very pleased.

Additionally, be smart and don't be influenced by reviews from people who are not "verified" purchasers of the product.

5.0 out of 5 starsThis Samsung TV sells itself
ByMark in Pa.on December 6, 2015
Size: 40-InchStyle: TV
I replaced my 10 year old SONY 40 inch LCD TV with this Samsung 40 inch LED smart TV. I reused the same wall mount bracket that the SONY hung on for years. The back of the SONY was flat and the Samsung has a protrusion on the bottom so I had to use spacers that I saved from the wall mount kit. I had to buy longer screws at a hardware store as the kits screws weren't long enough. Both TV's use M8 screws and the Samsung comes with spacers but, they weren't wide enough. With the light dim in the room and the thin frame of this TV it looks like it's floating on the wall.
From the second I opened the box I knew this was a good purchase. When electronics work "right" the second you turn it on you know you got a good one. This TV is perfect in "every way" and has been playing for three days straight. I always do a "50 hour burn in" with any computer or TV. The color is so precise I'm not going to calibrate it either. I have the calibration disc but, I feel no need to adjust this TV's color. The TV is talking to both my laptop's which are running windows 10. I've signed in to HBOGO, VUDU, and Flixster. I've had my family over to watch HBO boxing and they were amazed with this TV. This Samsung TV sells it self.
